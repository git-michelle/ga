Uganda is one of the poorest countries on the African continent and in the world. More than one third of all young children – 2.4 million – are stunted.
Many of the long-term damages that children endure due to this environment are irreversible.

With your support, Gift Angels Foundation, will be able to provide the necessary services and items to fight against the malnourishment problem in Uganda’s youth.
Donation funds are used to provide Uganda’s youth with necessities, such as food, shelter, medical care, and access to education.

Gift Angels strives to be completely transparent with all donors on where and how donated funds are used.
Please use our contact form if you'd like your donations to be used for a specific purpose.
We believe every child deserves a safe and nurturing environment as they grow, but sadly, in Uganda, the harsh environment doesn’t allow for safety or nourishment.
We appreciate and thank all donors.
Please know your contribution makes a difference in someone’s life and brings us closer to our goals.

Initiatives




  NUTRITION & HEALTH

Your support will allow us to provide proper health care and necessities such as food and water.



  EDUCATION

We aim to provide educational services from health education to increasing the overall level of youth literacy.



  SUPPORT FOR HIV POSITIVE

Our current projects include awareness campaigns about prevention methods and the necessary medicines to fight the disease.



  WATER & SANITATION

Water and sanitation are essential for a healthy life. Uganda faces an issue with poor sanitation and hygiene education, which leaves children at risk of getting ill. We want to change the way children access drinking water and provide the necessary education relating to hygiene/sanitation.



  AGRICULTURE

At present, Uganda is unable to provide and buy quality crops. With your help, quality crops will be purchased and cultivated to help meet the basic needs of poverty-stricken individuals.



  COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT

Those who live in poverty-stricken communities have difficulty receiving the goods needed to live a healthy life. Your contribution will help to develop sanitation facilities, infrastructure, disaster readiness, and more.

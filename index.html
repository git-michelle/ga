Gift Angels Overview

Gift Angels Foundation is a non-profit organization, with a focus on providing necessary services for the impacted communities in Uganda.
We are involved in a range of activities which include, education , agriculture, health , community development programs, and support of HIV positive street children.
We are a registered non-profit organization that has impacted various communities in Uganda since 2015.
